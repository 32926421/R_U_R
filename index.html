About us

Really Useful Recyclers was born in the wonderfully creative heart and mind of Deb Flintoff – bringing together two great passions of her son Joshua – Disney Pixar’s Wall-E the Recycling Robot and Thomas the Tank Engine who is really useful. These passions were the inspiration behind the creation of a small business enterprise, Really Useful Recyclers – where, from that small seed a beautiful vision grew. Josh began first recycling used newspaper and packaging, and then began creation of unique artwork from recycled newspaper. Soon after, Deb and Josh invited Courtney Smith to join this really useful enterprise team – Courtney also has loved Wall-E and Thomas his whole life.
